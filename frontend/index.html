Water Quality Index (WQI) Methodology

WQI Formula: WQI = Σ (Wi × qi)
Where qi = [(Ci - Smin) / (Smax - Smin)] × 100
Wi = weight of parameter i
Parameter CPCB Standard (Max) Weight
